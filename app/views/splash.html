How the service works

Access curriculum resources is a service for teachers. It enables them to access their school’s curriculum online, including all the lesson plans, resources, and subject knowledge they will need to teach it.

The Department for Education has collaborated with educational experts and cultural institutions to create a service that aims to reduce teacher workloads and enhance the quality of teaching in schools, with all teaching materials available to download or print from the service.

Curriculum content has been sequenced so that the knowledge and vocabulary pupils gain at school can be retained and applied later in life.
