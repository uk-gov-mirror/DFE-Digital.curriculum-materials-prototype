Create and plan lessons online

Teachers can use this service to create and plan lessons online using resources designed by curriculum providers.

Use this service to:

  • create and plan lessons in one place
  • access resources tailored to your school’s curriculum
  • manage lesson content by term, subject and year group

Resources

There are currently resources available for:

  • key stages 1 and 2 maths and science
  • key stages 2 and 3 history and geography

You’ll need a valid school email address to sign up.

This service is only available in England.

Before you start

Your school can use this service if it is:

  • maintained or local authority funded
  • an academy or academy trust
  • a free school
