Part 1: 10 minutes

You can choose one resource to replace the suggested one.

video 1min 30s

Description

A video of Mount Kilauea, Hawaii (an active, shield volcano) erupting in 2019.

Pupils apply their prior knowledge of the different types of volcanoes, focussing on the effects of volcanic eruptions.

Ask pupils to watch the video carefully, noting down as many words as they can about what they observe. Remind them of their last lesson to help them. Follow up with a discussion to check answers and test vocabulary.
