Access your school’s curriculum online

Teachers can use this service to access their school’s complete curriculum online.

You can access the full course of study and its resources, lesson by lesson.

Use this service to:

  • view your curriculum’s contents by year and by subject
  • organise all lessons, using plans and resources tailored to the curriculum
  • find helpful guidance on teaching the curriculum

Resources

Resources are currently available for:

  • key stages 1 and 2 maths and science
  • key stages 2 and 3 history and geography

You’ll need a valid school email address to sign up.

This service is only available in England.
