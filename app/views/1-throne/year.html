Year 6 geography curriculum

The locational, topographical and meteorological knowledge developed earlier in the Key Stage acts as a great foundation for the knowledge and skills to be built in future years. Themes developed over the course of multiple units include the water cycle, climate and change, urbanisation and migration, environmental interactions, global perspectives, and landscape systems.

Pupils will focus on their map skills, and learn to record and communicate their fieldwork using a range of methods, in different contexts across the year.
