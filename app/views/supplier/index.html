Provide complete curriculum programmes

Curriculum designers can use this service to provide complete curriculum programmes to the Department for Education (DfE).

Use this service to:

  • provide complete curriculum programmes, step by step
  • understand how to provide content in line with Government Digital Service (GDS) standards
  • preview how your curriculum content will be used by teachers
  • publish your curriculum content with the DfE

You’ll be able to save your progress as you provide programme content and return to the service when you’re ready to add more.

This service is only available in England.
